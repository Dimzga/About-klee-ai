What is AMV?

AMV (Anime Music Video) is a combination of clips from various types of anime that are combined into one that is adapted to music.

What is JEDAG JEDUG?

Jedag jedug or what is often called by young people, JJ, is video content that is instrumental in the sound of remixed music.
